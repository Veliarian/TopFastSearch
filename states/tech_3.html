Free Online Tools to Grow Your Business

Streamlining certain tasks using the internet can help you spend more time on growth. Here’s a rundown of some free online tools you can utilize to grow your business.

Hootsuite

Being on social media is no longer optional for businesses, making simplified social media management necessary. Hootsuite is one such social media management tool you can use to manage all your social media accounts in one dashboard. Hootsuite’s free package has many features you can employ to engage with your audience and monitor your social accounts’ performance and growth. There is also an auto-schedule feature you can use to plan all your social media posts well in advance, helping you save time for other daily tasks.

ZOHO Projects

ZOHO Projects is a cloud-based tool that lets teams work efficiently by collaborating. The free version has several features, including those of assigning tasks, organizing projects, and setting priority levels for certain tasks. Additionally, there is also an option of integrating programs like Google Apps and Dropbox.

Due

Due provides solutions for billing and payments. It helps speed up payments and increase cash flow. Moreover, you can keep track of your projects’ timeline, and get an overview of your staff, clients, and projects. This helps you get a better idea of the business’s productivity and how you can enhance it. Due also provides tools that can help you send and receive payments more quickly and conveniently, along with payment options like ACH, PayPal integration, a digital wallet, domestic and international card processing, and eCash transactions.

Groove

Bad customer support reflects poorly on any business. Missing even a single email can lead to dissatisfied customers posting unfavorable online reviews about your organization. You can stay on top of your customer support by using Groove, a free help desk software.

Groove allows you to convert your existing email into a professional help desk for customer service. In addition to helping you stay organized, this makes your business appear more professional. There is also an option to integrate features like live chat, social media, SMS, and phone, helping you manage everything related to customer service using a single tool.

While running a business, there are so many aspects on which you need to focus. Use these online tools to utilize your time in a manner that helps your business grow better.
