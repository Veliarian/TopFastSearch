Samsung Galaxy Phones that You Should Consider Buying

Samsung’s Galaxy product line this year boasts innovative, efficient smartphones with industry-driven features, Artificial Intelligence and sleek designs. Here’s a quick round up of the best from their 2020 lineup.

Samsung Galaxy S20 and Galaxy S20+

With their high RAM storage and streamlined waterproof bodies these 5G compatible phones are the easy choice if you can afford them. The AI powered camera has a massive zoom range and takes high res pictures and video.

Samsung Galaxy S20 Ultra

This phone takes its cues from the S20 and S20 + but the camera with its 100X Super Resolution Zoom is where it really shines. The future proof technology, long battery life, big screen and display makes this phone worth the high price point.

Samsung Galaxy Note10 Android Smartphone

If you’re a gamer, you’ll love the powerful gesture control, huge display panel and S Pen stylus on the Samsung Galaxy Note10 Android Smartphone. Just like all former Note products, this phone does away with the audio jack and the Bixby button.

Samsung Galaxy S10+ Android Smartphone

This phone has an Infinity Super AMOLED display, five built-in cameras (three rear-facing and two front-facing) and is an affordable buy. A variant within this category is the Samsung Galaxy S10 which has an even lower price point, a 6.1 inch display and a smaller battery.

Samsung Galaxy A50 Android Smartphone

For an extremely affordable price, you get a Super AMOLED display, a triple camera, fingerprint sensor and a powerful battery life.

Samsung Galaxy Fold Android Smartphone

The most exciting feature in the Samsung Galaxy Fold Android Smartphone is the 7.3-inch Dynamic AMOLED panel which folds down to turn this phone into a tablet. Though the body isn’t water resistant, it does come with an aramid fiber cover and Galaxy wireless buds.

Samsung Galaxy Z Flip Android Smartphone

The foldable display on this phone splits the interface of certain built-in apps which makes it perfect for people who like to multitask. The 6.7 inch Dynamic AMOLED display on this model is made from flexible glass.

Samsung Galaxy S10e Unlocked Smartphone

This Samsung Galaxy model has a borderless Infinity Super AMOLED display, expandable storage and between 6 to 8 GB of RAM, depending which variant you buy.
