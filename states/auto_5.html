SUVs Versus Sedans: A Comparison

SUVs and sedans are the two most popular auto segments across the country. Even if you prefer one over the other, you should consider all factors to determine which type is a better fit for your needs. Here is the information you need to know. 

SUV Advantages

Space: This is the biggest advantage of an SUV. The sheer amount of space in one makes it great for carrying passengers or cargo. Passengers will have a lot of space to stretch out and be comfortable on long drives. Cargo capacity in all SUVs is also impressive. 

Capabilities: SUVs are easily more capable than sedans. All-wheel drive is often standard on SUVs. This makes them safe and reliable if you often drive through hazardous weather or off-road. 

Visibility: The driver sits high up in an SUV. This offers them excellent visibility of the road ahead. The large windows and rear windshield also enhance the surrounding views. Drives are safer when the driver can see better. 

Sedan Advantages

Safety: Sedans are usually safer than SUVs because new safety features often debut on sedans. The closed trunk also offers a safe and private space for storing valuables. 

Fuel Economy: Sedans are always more fuel-efficient than equivalent SUVs. Most are powered by an efficient four-cylinder engine. Even the ones with a V6 or V8 are more efficient because sedans are lighter than SUVs. 

Drivability: Sedans are much easier to drive than SUVs because they have a low center of gravity. SUVs tend to roll a lot. Cornering and parking in a sedan are much more manageable. 

Budget: Sedans are more affordable than equivalent SUVs. Even if money is not much of an issue, you can get more luxury and more features in sedans for less money than an SUV.
